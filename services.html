Safety & Compliance

Your Safety is Our Top Priority – Always

Zero Lost-Time Accidents – 5 Consecutive Years

Our unwavering commitment to safety has resulted in **zero lost-time incidents** for over 5 years across all active sites. This remarkable safety record is not luck — it's the outcome of rigorous planning, continuous training, strict compliance, and a genuine culture of care for every team member.

Our Safety Standards & Practices

  • OHSAS 18001 / ISO 45001 Certified – Fully compliant with international occupational health & safety management systems
  • Mandatory Safety Training – Every worker receives 40+ hours of annual safety training (including first aid, fire safety, working at heights, electrical hazards, and heavy machinery operation)
  • Full PPE Provision – Hard hats, safety boots, high-visibility vests, gloves, harnesses, respirators, ear protection — provided and enforced at all sites
  • Daily Safety Briefings & Audits – Tool-box talks every morning + weekly site safety inspections by certified safety officers
  • Risk Assessments & Method Statements – Every task is pre-assessed with detailed RAMS (Risk Assessment & Method Statement) approved before start
  • Incident Reporting & Near-Miss Culture – Anonymous reporting system + rewards for reporting near-misses to prevent future incidents
  • Emergency Preparedness – On-site first-aid stations, trained first-aiders, fire extinguishers, evacuation drills every quarter

Safety is not just a policy — it's our core value. We believe every worker should return home safely to their family every single day.
